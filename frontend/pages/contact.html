FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available at checkout. Free standard shipping on all orders over $50.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied, contact us and we'll arrange a full refund or exchange.

Are all products really cruelty-free?

Absolutely. Every single product is certified cruelty-free by Leaping Bunny. We will never test on animals.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 40 countries. International shipping rates and times vary by destination.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number by email. You can also view all orders in your account page.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placing them. Contact us immediately and we'll do our best to help.
