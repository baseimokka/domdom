How It Started

Born from a Love of Beauty & Ethics

DomDom Store was founded in 2020 with one simple mission: make premium beauty products that never compromise on ethics. Our founder, passionate about makeup since childhood, was frustrated by the lack of truly cruelty-free, high-performance options on the market.

She spent two years working with leading cosmetic chemists and ethical sourcing experts to create a brand that delivers on both performance and principles. Every formula is tested on willing human volunteers — never animals.

Today, DomDom serves over 50,000 customers worldwide, and we're just getting started.
