⚙️ Store Settings

ℹ️ About Payment Settings

These values are displayed to the customer during checkout when they select InstaPay or Vodafone Cash as payment method.


InstaPay Account Name — the name the customer should transfer to.

InstaPay Phone / Username — the phone number or username linked to your InstaPay account.

Vodafone Cash Number — the Vodafone Cash wallet number.

Payment Instructions — an optional note shown below the account details.

Free Shipping Threshold — orders with a subtotal at or above this amount get free shipping. Updates checkout and the storefront banners instantly.

ℹ️ These banners appear as a slider on the homepage, below the main hero section. Only active banners are displayed, sorted by order (lowest first). The static hero above them is permanent and cannot be managed here.

Hero Banners
